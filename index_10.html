What is new streamlined pathway?
The streamlined pathway (introduced in 2025) is an easier and faster way for experienced overseas nurses to get Australia AHPRA registration without NCLEX and OSCE exams.

Important note: This tool is designed for candidates who have completed Nursing from India.
